Healthy Lifestyle and Prevention

Nutrition Guidelines:
- Eat a balanced diet with variety of fruits and vegetables
- Include whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats
- Limit processed foods, added sugars, and sodium
- Stay hydrated (8 glasses of water daily)
- Practice portion control
- Eat regular meals, don't skip breakfast

Exercise Recommendations:
- Adults: 150 minutes moderate aerobic activity per week
- Include strength training 2 days per week
- Start slowly if new to exercise
- Choose activities you enjoy
- Exercise with a friend for motivation
- Consult doctor before starting new exercise program

Sleep Hygiene:
- Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night
- Maintain consistent sleep schedule
- Create relaxing bedtime routine
- Keep bedroom dark, quiet, and cool
- Avoid screens 1 hour before bed
- Limit caffeine after 2 PM

Preventive Health Screenings:
- Annual physical examination
- Blood pressure check annually
- Cholesterol screening every 5 years
- Diabetes screening every 3 years (if at risk)
- Colonoscopy starting at age 45
- Mammogram for women starting at age 40
- Dental checkup every 6 months
- Eye examination every 1-2 years
